## What I'm reading 
**[The Literary Network: Essays on Compositions and Authors, Works and Artists](https://www.thelitnet.com/)** by Noa Manheim - A free-spirited collection of essays full of love for western literature, traveling between eras and authors. The main idea is to present surprising connections between ideas, people and moments. Imagine a combination between gossip and in-depth commentary about writers and writing.  

**[There Is No Antimemetics Division](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/54870256-there-is-no-antimemetics-division)** by qntm — A mind-bending exploration of ideas that resist being remembered or understood. Action, relationships, great fantasy that involved philosophy. You had me at the first sentence.
