## What is a now page?

This is a [/now page](https://nownownow.com/about) — an idea by [Derek Sivers](https://sive.rs/nowff).
Unlike an "about" page that covers who you are, a /now page covers what you're
focused on *right now* — updated occasionally, not hourly.
