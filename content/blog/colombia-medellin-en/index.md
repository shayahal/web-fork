## Colombia Trip: Medellín

### The city of he-who-must-not-be-named

### A wonderful city nestled in a green valley between mountains

Medellín is a wonderful city nestled in a green valley between mountains. Neighborhoods become progressively poorer at higher elevations.

### The History and Transformation

This city was the most dangerous in the world during the 1980s when Pablo Escobar controlled it violently. Today's residents are actively recovering, and the city projects positivity through urban renewal and welcoming locals.

### What to Do

- **Comuna 13** - A colorful, picturesque neighborhood with heavy tourist presence
- **Jardín Botánico de Medellín** - The botanical gardens
- **Museum of Antioquia** - An art museum
- **Food tour in Envigado neighborhood** - Taste local cuisine
- **Cable car rides** - Scenic panoramic views

### Restaurants and Bars

**Amor-Acuyá Gelato** - Exceptional gelato in the city center

**Sambombi Bistró Local** - An excellent restaurant in Provenza neighborhood

**Carmen Medellín** - A Michelin-recommended venue with outstanding cuisine and cocktails

**Café Zorba** - A local recommendation with excellent pizza and live musicians

**Alambique** - A bar-restaurant worth making a reservation for, featuring the special Viche-sour cocktail made from a rare spirit produced only on Colombia's Pacific coast
