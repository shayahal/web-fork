# Generic Packing List

That's my packing list. Enjoy! 

## Documents
- Passport
- Passport photocopy
- Flight tickets
- Travel insurance
- Credit cards
- Cash
- Driver's license
- International driver's license

## Equipment
- Water bottle
- Sunglasses
- Spare sunglasses
- Spare glasses
- Phone charger
- Power adapter
- Reading book
- Kindle + charger
- Wireless earphones
- Wired earphones
- iPad
- Lip balm
- Moisturizer
- Ear plugs
- Hat

## Clothing
- Light long pants
- Shorts 
- Short-sleeve shirts 
- Light long-sleeve shirt 
- Light fleece
- Rain jacket
- Windbreaker
- Swimwear 
- Socks
- Underwear 
- Pajamas
- Hiking pants

## Shoes
- Comfortable walking shoes for treks
- Sports shoes
- Flip-flops / sandals
- Nicer shoes for evening

## Toiletries
- Toothbrush and toothpaste
- Shampoo and soap
- Deodorant
- Travel perfume
- Medications

## Additional Items
- Laundry bag
- Hand sanitizer gel
- Band-aids
- Sunscreen
- Tissues
- Neck pillow

---

## Pre-Travel Checklist
- [ ] Check-in for flight
- [ ] Order cash for airport
- [ ] Print travel insurance
- [ ] Print hotel confirmations
- [ ] Set up eSIM

**Tip:** Print this list and check off each item as you pack it. This will help ensure you don't forget anything!
